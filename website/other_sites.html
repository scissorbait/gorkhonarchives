Other fanblogs and fansites

There aren't a whole lot of sites I've seen dedicated to Pathologic, but whenever I do track some down I will put them here!

Pathologistics [archive] - A neat little blog where two guys talk about their experiences playing the original 2005 release of Pathologic. I haven't read through every post (yet) but they're written in a very fun style. They only talk about the Bachelor and Haruspex routes, but apparently there used to be a separate blog for the Changeling (or Devotress) route that is now defunct. If anyone has an archive of that, then please let me know!

Pathologic the Musical - A blog dedicated to a musical version of Pathologic that one of my friends have been working on. The songs they have written for it are great, go give their work a peek :^)

Snapshots From Gorkhon - "a collection of screenshots from pathologic classic HD and pathologic 2."

Клара & the boyz - A sort of visual 'archive' of Pathologic related content. This site owes a lot to this blog for having so many high resolution images :'^)

Who Are You? - A neat artistic visualization of the Pathologic character select screen!

Patho Classic Cutting Room Floor - A text-based site that has a lot of interesting info laid out that was datamined from different versions of the original Pathologic.
